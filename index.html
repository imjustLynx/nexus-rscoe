HTML Quiz Battle

During the HTML Quiz Battle Event, participants engaged in an exciting competition designed to test their knowledge of HTML concepts and coding skills. The event featured multiple rounds, starting with a preliminary quiz where contestants answered a series of challenging questions covering various HTML topics, including elements, attributes, and best practices. the event fostered a spirit of learning and camaraderie among aspiring web developers, making it a memorable experience for all involved.
